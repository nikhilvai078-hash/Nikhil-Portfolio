## 🌟 About This Portfolio

A modern, professional, and visually appealing personal portfolio website showcasing:
- Nikhil's journey as a B.Com student preparing for CAT
- Professional experience in banking and insurance
- Content creation and video editing accomplishments
- Passion for technology and gaming
- Interactive and engaging design with modern aesthetics

---

## 📋 Features

### ✨ Design & User Experience
- **Dark Modern Theme** with technology-inspired aesthetics
- **Premium Black Background** with blue accent colors
- **Glassmorphism UI Elements** with subtle futuristic design
- **Smooth Animations & Hover Effects** for premium feel
- **Fully Responsive Design** optimized for all devices
- **Mobile-Friendly Navigation** with hamburger menu
- **High-Performance** optimized loading and rendering

### 🎯 Key Sections

1. **Hero Section** - Professional profile picture with animated backgrounds
2. **About Me** - Personal overview and educational background
3. **Experience** - Bajaj Finserv internship details
4. **Skills** - 6 core skill areas with icons
5. **Projects** - Jelly Bear YouTube Channel showcase
6. **Achievements** - Animated statistics
7. **Contact** - Multiple contact options and form
8. **Footer** - Navigation and social links
